ABOUT

Thomas Cruise Mapother IV

Born on July 3, 1962, Tom Cruise is one of Hollywood's most recognizable and highest-paid actors. Known for his charismatic screen presence and dedication to performing his own stunts, Cruise has starred in over 40 films and has received numerous accolades throughout his career spanning four decades.

Cruise first rose to fame with roles in Risky Business (1983) and Top Gun (1986), establishing himself as a leading man in Hollywood. His career continued to flourish with critically acclaimed performances in Rain Man (1988), A Few Good Men (1992), and Jerry Maguire (1996), the latter earning him a Golden Globe Award and Academy Award nomination.

In recent years, Cruise has become synonymous with the Mission: Impossible franchise, performing increasingly daring stunts that have become legendary in Hollywood. His commitment to his craft and his ability to consistently deliver box office hits have cemented his status as one of the most bankable stars in cinema history.
